Beef and Broccoli

Description

Beef and Broccoli is one of the most popular dishes on Chinese restaurant menus. There are many beef and broccoli recipes on the Internet, with variations made in slow cookers, in Instant Pots, and on sheet pans.

Ingredients

For the beef and marinade:

  • 1 pound flank steak
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce

For the sauce:

  • 2/3 cup low sodium chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon white pepper

For the rest of the dish:

  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
